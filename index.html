About Me

Hi, I am a 4th-year PhD Candiate in Robotics Institute, ECE Department, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. My supervisor is Prof. Fang Lu.

Meanwhile, I work closely with Prof. Yebin Liu at Tsinghua University since 2016. I also spent 10 months working with Prof. Christian Theobalt at Max-Planck-Institute for Informatics, Saarbrucken, Germany.


My research topics include real-time dynamic and static 3D reconstruction, Human Performance Capture, 3D modeling and Aerial Robot.

My goal is to enable convenient and high quality performance capture in our daily life.

  • A hybrid asynchronous optimization and refinement scheme.
  • An event camera-based dataset with fast human motion capture results at 1000 fps
